The COP 26 Agreement
Environment:
- Recognizing the emergency: Reaffirmation of the Paris Agreement goal to limit global temperature increase, expressing concern about current warming levels and the need for urgent action.
- Accelerating action: Emphasis on the critical decade and the call for stronger national action plans to achieve net-zero emissions by mid-century.
- Moving away from fossil fuels: Agreement on a phase-down of coal power and a phase-out of "inefficient" fossil fuel subsidies.
- Completing the Paris rulebook: Agreement on operational details for the practical implementation of the Paris Agreement, including norms related to carbon markets.
