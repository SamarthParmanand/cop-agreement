The COP 27 Agreement
Environment (E):
Loss and Damage Funding: Breakthrough agreement at COP27 to provide funding for countries affected by climate disasters.
Mitigation Work Programme: Established to urgently scale up mitigation ambition and implementation to limit global temperature rise to 1.5°C.
Global Stocktake: A process where countries discuss progress towards Paris Agreement goals. Key findings to be presented at COP28.
Coal Power Phase-down: Governments urged to accelerate efforts towards the phasedown of unabated coal power.
Renewable Energy: Stress on the importance of a clean energy mix, including low-emission and renewable energy.
