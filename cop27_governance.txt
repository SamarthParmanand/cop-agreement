The COP 27 Agreement
Governance (G):
Transparency and Accountability: Priority in 2023 to ensure transparency and accountability with non-state actors, including businesses and institutions.
Climate Finance: Pathway created at COP27 to align finance flows towards low emissions and climate-resilient development.
Climate Finance Goals: Deliberations on setting a new collective quantified goal on climate finance in 2024, considering the needs of developing countries.
Green Climate Fund: Call for developed countries to provide resources for the second replenishment of the Green Climate Fund.
Adaptation Finance: Standing Committee on Finance tasked with preparing a report on doubling adaptation finance for consideration at COP28.
