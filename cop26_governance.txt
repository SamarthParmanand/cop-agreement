The COP 26 Agreement
Governance:
- Forests: Commitment by 137 countries to halt and reverse forest loss and land degradation by 2030, backed by significant public and private funding.
- Methane: Global Methane Pledge signed by 103 countries to limit methane emissions by 30% by 2030, with a focus on reducing a potent greenhouse gas.
- Cars: Declaration by over 30 countries and major vehicle manufacturers for all new car and van sales to be zero-emission vehicles by 2040 globally and 2035 in leading markets.
- Coal: Groundbreaking partnership to support South Africa's transition away from coal, with financial aid provided by leaders from various countries and the European Union.
- Private finance: Private financial institutions and central banks announcing moves to realign trillions of dollars towards achieving global net-zero emissions, including initiatives like the Glasgow Financial Alliance for Net Zero.
