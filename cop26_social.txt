The COP 26 Agreement
Social:
- Delivering on climate finance: Urging developed countries to fulfill the promise of delivering US$100 billion a year for developing countries.
- Stepping up support for adaptation: Calls for a doubling of finance to support developing countries in adapting to climate change impacts and building resilience.
- Focusing on loss & damage: Strengthening the Santiago Network to address climate risks and launching a new "Glasgow dialogue" to discuss funding for activities related to loss and damage.
